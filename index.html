Free Email Validator Tool

Welcome to our Free Email Validator. This simple tool helps you quickly check if email addresses are syntactically valid and if their domains have mail servers configured. It is designed to be fast, easy-to-use, and free for everyone.

Our service does not save or store any email addresses you enter. We respect your privacy and handle every validation in real-time strictly within your browser. No data leaves your computer except for DNS queries needed to confirm domain mail servers.

This tool is built for the community, by the community — helping individuals, developers, and businesses cleanse email lists, verify addresses, and improve email deliverability, all without any cost or signup required.

About Our Validator

    

      Our validation process includes:
      
    
        
  • Checking the basic email format using proven regular expressions.
    • 
        
  • Verifying the presence of mail servers for the domain using MX DNS record lookups.
    • 
        
  • Blocking well-known disposable email providers often used for temporary emails.
    • 
        
  • Flagging role-based email addresses like admin@, support@, and info@.
    • 
        
  • Ensuring email parts meet standard length requirements.
    • 
      

    


    

      Please note that this validator does not guarantee mailbox existence or that the email actually receives mail, as that would require checking servers via SMTP which cannot be done here due to privacy, security, and technical reasons.
    


    

      We continuously update our disposable domain blocklist and strive to maintain a reliable free tool accessible to everyone. Please use responsibly and respect fair usage.
    


    


    
Privacy & Security

    

      We take your privacy seriously. No emails or email lists are logged, saved, or transmitted beyond what is necessary for DNS MX record lookups. DNS queries are made only to public resolvers like Google DNS to verify domain mail servers.
